# 🚀 Customer Behavior Dashboard

An advanced *data visualization and analytics dashboard* designed to uncover deep insights into customer behavior, purchasing patterns, and revenue trends. This project transforms raw transactional data into *actionable business intelligence* through intuitive and interactive visualizations.

---

## 🌟 Overview

Understanding customer behavior is critical for business growth. This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of customer demographics, buying habits, and performance metrics, enabling data-driven decision-making.

It combines *data analysis, segmentation, and visualization techniques* to deliver meaningful insights in a clean and interactive interface.

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

* 👥 *Total Customers:* 392
* 💰 *Average Purchase Value:* $60.76
* ⭐ *Average Customer Rating:* 3.76

---

## 🔍 Core Features

* 📌 *Customer Segmentation* (Age, Gender, Subscription Status)
* 🛒 *Purchase Behavior Analysis*
* 📈 *Revenue & Sales Breakdown by Category*
* 🎯 *Age Group Performance Insights*
* 🚚 *Shipping Preference Analysis*
* ⚡ *Interactive Filters for Dynamic Exploration*

---

## 📈 Business Insights

* Adults contribute the *highest share of revenue and sales*
* Accessories category leads in *overall performance*
* Majority of customers are *non-subscribers*, indicating growth opportunity
* Customer distribution across age groups shows *balanced engagement patterns*

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* *Data Analysis:* Python (Pandas, NumPy)
* *Visualization:* Power BI / Tableau
* *Data Handling:* Excel / CSV / SQL
* *Concepts:* Data Analytics, Business Intelligence, Customer Segmentation

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

The primary goal of this project is to demonstrate how *data can be leveraged to understand customer behavior*, optimize business strategies, and enhance customer experience through visual storytelling.
